# 🎮 TGJ2O – Grade 10 Communications Technology  
## 🏁 Culminating Project: Board Game Development

---

## 🧠 Overview

Board games have been a favourite pastime for thousands of years, with some of today’s most popular games dating back as far as 3,000 B.C.E. Even with video games, streaming services, and other forms of entertainment, board games remain a popular, budget-friendly way to spend time with others.

During the pandemic, board games saw a major resurgence, as people looked for ways to stay entertained indoors. In 2020 alone, the board game market grew by 20%, and by 2023, revenue from board game sales was expected to reach $12 billion.  
Source: https://www.fun.com/best-selling-board-games-all-time.html

Throughout this course, you have developed skills across multiple areas of communications technology. This culminating project brings those skills together into one cohesive, real-world style product.

You will design and create a completely original board game from scratch. Using AI to help generate and refine ideas, you will move through the full design process from concept to final production.

Your final product should be unique, creative, and reflect both your technical skills and your ability to plan, design, and execute a complete project.

## ⚖️ Structure vs Creativity

Unlike the open project system you have been working in, this culminating project includes **more structure, checkpoints, and clear expectations**.

This is intentional.

At this stage of the course, the goal is to:
- 🧩 Bring together **all of your skills** into one cohesive project  
- 📊 Demonstrate your learning clearly across **KTCA categories**  
- ⏱️ Manage your time effectively within a **set production schedule**  
- 🧪 Create something that can be **fully completed, tested, and refined**  

With a shared project format, expectations are more consistent, making it easier to:
- Assess your work fairly  
- Support you throughout the process  
- Ensure everyone produces a complete final product  

That said, there is still **a lot of room for creativity and choice**.

You will make decisions around:
- 🎨 Theme and concept  
- 🎮 Game mechanics and rules  
- 🖼️ Visual design and layout  
- 🧱 3D models and physical components  
- 🎬 Style and tone of your commercial  

The structure provides the framework.  
Your creativity is what brings the project to life.

## 🛠️ What You Will Create

You are responsible for designing and producing the following components:

- 🧩 **Game name, theme, and rules**  
  *(Planned with AI using a provided prompt)*  

- 🖼️ **Game board**  
  *(Adobe Photoshop)*  

- 🃏 **Game cards and printable rules sheet**  
  *(Adobe Photoshop)*  

- 🧱 **Game tokens and pieces**  
  *(Tinkercad + 3D printing)*  

- 🎬 **30-second video commercial**  
  *(Adobe Premiere Pro)*

## 🎯 Final Outcome

Once all elements are complete:

- 🖨️ Your game will be **printed, assembled, and tested in class**
- 🎲 You will **playtest each other’s games**
- 🔁 You will **refine your rules based on real gameplay**
- 💭 You will **reflect on how your AI-generated ideas held up in practice**

This project is not about recreating an existing game.  
It is about designing something original that demonstrates your creativity, problem-solving, and technical ability.
