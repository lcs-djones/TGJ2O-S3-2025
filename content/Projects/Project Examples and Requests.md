# 🧭 Project Ideas, Requests, and Important Dates

This mod is designed to give you **maximum freedom** to pursue your own creative project.

Most students should be **coming up with their own idea**.  
This page is mainly for:
- Students who are **truly stuck**
- Projects that the school has **specifically requested**

If you already have a strong idea, you do **not** need to use anything from this page.
